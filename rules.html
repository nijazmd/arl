Points are awarded based on both the finishing position and the number of chances taken.

1. Race Format

  • Each race consists of up to 5 chances per driver.
  • Drivers must complete at least one full lap per chance.
  • Fastest clean lap determines final standing.

2. Car and Track Selection

  • Car selections are pre-approved and categorized by performance class.
  • Track and circuit rotations follow a fixed schedule.
